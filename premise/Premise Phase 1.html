The principles
  1. The number is the hero.Tabular JetBrains Mono. No jitter. Decimals de-emphasized.
  2. Every label earns its place.Monospaced eyebrows signal "this is measured, not decorative."
  3. Grid, not float.1px rules do the work that shadows do elsewhere. Calm, not soft.
  4. Color has function.Green = bullish, red = bearish, petrol = the house. Nothing else gets color.
  5. Data density without density.7 / 10 on the density slider — Bloomberg honesty, Linear restraint.
  6. One typographic voice.Display (General Sans) × Body (Inter) × Numerals (JetBrains Mono). Serif is gone.
