THE MORNING BRIEF3 MIN · LF + MK

A quiet rotation out of duration into cyclicals.

Last week closed the way we expected — the break-out in semis held on confirming volume, and the rotation into European industrials we flagged on April 8 is now consensus. The interesting tell is not the rally, but the dispersion: winners won clean, laggards stayed bid. That's a regime, not a squeeze.

Three things to watch this week. Nvidia enters our 820–860 zone — we accumulate. The ECB speaks Wednesday; we expect a dovish surprise. And the first US 30Y auction since the pivot prints Thursday — if it stops through, the duration trade is finally back.
